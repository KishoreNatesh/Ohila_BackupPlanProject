Ohila

INDEPENDENT MUSICIAN, VOCALIST, GUITARIST, NATIONAL SWIMMER, PHILANTHROPIST

Ohileshwari is an Indian composer, singer, music producer, musician multi instrumentalist, Swimmer and philanthropist.

Her works are notable for integrating Eastern classical music with world music genres and traditional orchestral arrangements.

She has won several titles in reality shows and has won many medals in swimming.

Skilled in Hindustani Classical music her compositions have won accolades from both young and old alike.
